What the redesign decides

Ruhe als Default

The week shows only the accepted plan and the now-line. Reality, ghosts, life and capacity — and the backlog rail — live behind layer chips, one tap each, persisted per user. Never all loud at once.

Colour as an edge

Project colour identifies a block on its left edge, never as a fill. Fills stay neutral surfaces, so ten blocks read as calm structure instead of a paint chart.

Zeit-Kompression

Unplanned edge hours (≈0–7 and 20–24) collapse to a thin band via the pure compressWindow; a block at 06:30 keeps its whole hour. Blocks gain height and legibility.

Sevi's empty stage

A truly empty planner is not a dead wall: three questions produce the first ghost week through the plan-apply seam (planner-firstrun), one tap accepts. No demo data, ever.
