What the redesign decides

Ruhe als Default

The week shows only the accepted plan and the now-line. Reality, ghosts, life and capacity — and the backlog rail — live behind layer chips, one tap each, persisted per user. Never all loud at once.

Farbe knallt, Ruhe kommt aus Layern

Project colour is the block's bold fill, punchy on both sides — calm comes from closing layers and compressing edge hours, not from draining the colour. Text ink is picked by luminance so contrast holds AA on every fill.

Zeit-Kompression

Unplanned edge hours (≈0–7 and 20–24) collapse to a thin band via the pure compressWindow; a block at 06:30 keeps its whole hour. Blocks gain height and legibility — a 30–60 min block never squashes to a sliver.

Sevi's empty stage

A truly empty planner is not a dead wall: three questions produce the first ghost week through the plan-apply seam (planner-firstrun), one tap accepts. No demo data, ever.
